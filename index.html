About Me

I am currently summer intern in Samsung IOT lab in IIT Delhi for Celestini project India, a two-phase project led by Dr. Aakanksha Chowdhery (Princeton University and 2012 Marconi Society Young Scholar) and Prof. Brejesh Lall (IIT Delhi) as directors.
